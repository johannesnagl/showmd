AI writes in markdown.
The least you can do is render it.

unique to show.md

YAML frontmatter,
finally rendered.

Most markdown viewers ignore the --- block at the top of your files. show.md parses it and surfaces it as a collapsible metadata table — collapsed by default so it never gets in the way, always one click away when you need it.

Works with plain values, quoted strings, arrays, and multi-line folded blocks. Everything authors actually write.

what it does

⚡
Instant open

Set as default for .md files. Spacebar to preview in Finder. Zero friction.

⌘
macOS native

Feels like it belongs. Respects system fonts, dark mode, and your keyboard.

≡
Frontmatter rendering

YAML metadata rendered as a collapsible table. Collapsed by default, one click to expand. Unique to show.md.

◎
No bloat

Not an editor. Not a note app. Just a viewer. Opens in under a second.

⇄
Rendered & source

Switch between rendered and raw markdown with a single click. Copy as HTML anytime.

⬡
Private by default

No cloud. No uploads. No telemetry. Your files never leave your machine.
